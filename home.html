About Us

Our organization was founded in 2015, driven by our love for animals and our desire to bring justice because they also deserve a home, love and family.

The ways we provide help is through donations to provide more resources to follow our cause or through direct adoption.

  • Our goal is to help both homeless furry friends and people. Having a pet has been proven to improve overall human well-being.

  • We accept this as our mission, our duty. We think that since there is no one else to do it, it is our duty, which we are happy to fulfill. We believe we bring contagious positivity and zest.
